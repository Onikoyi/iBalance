# Product Requirements Document - iBalance

## Product Name
iBalance

## Product Category
Cloud-based, multi-tenant accounting platform

## Mission
To provide a secure, sector-ready accounting platform that supports multiple industries through a common finance core and configurable sector packs.

## Primary Target Market for v1
1. Private universities
2. Oil and gas servicing firms
3. Mid-sized finance and operations-driven businesses

## v1 Objectives
- Launch fast with a commercially usable accounting core
- Support multiple tenants securely
- Provide strong audit trail and accounting integrity
- Deliver sector differentiation through universities and oil & gas packs

## v1 Core Modules
- Identity and access management
- Tenant management
- Chart of Accounts
- General Ledger
- Journal posting
- Accounts Payable
- Accounts Receivable
- Cash and Bank
- Audit trail
- Reporting foundation

## v1 Sector Packs
### Universities
- Student receivables accounting
- Departmental budgets
- Grants and restricted funds
- Service billing support

### Oil & Gas
- Project cost tracking
- Cost centers by field/project
- Operational expense allocation
- Asset and cost analysis support

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Production-grade architecture
- Multi-tenant support
- Secure authentication and authorization
- Full auditability
- Reversible accounting corrections
- Scalable deployment model
- API-first design

## Commercial Objectives
- First pilot tenant as early as possible
- Template-driven onboarding
- Low-cost deployment for early market entry
